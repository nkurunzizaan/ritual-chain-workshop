# Reflection: Adding Staking to a Bounty System

Staking introduces economic incentives for honest behavior in bounty systems. Participants risk losing their stake if they don't reveal or if they lose – this discourages low-effort submissions.

**Public vs Hidden:** As with commit-reveal, answers stay hidden until reveal; staking amounts are public to ensure transparency.

**AI vs Human:** AI evaluates submissions objectively; the owner (human) finalizes the winner, controlling the slashing mechanism.

**Trade-off:** Staking creates a barrier to entry but filters out spam submissions. It also adds complexity to the contract, but the benefits of reducing spam outweigh the costs.
